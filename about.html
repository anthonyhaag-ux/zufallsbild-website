AHADIA is a group of spatial practitioners and researchers working together since 2018.

Seeking a balance with nature, projects take advantage of site-specific conditions. //005 The Ice Shell Project uses the phase change of water for creating thin shells out of ice, and //007 House Without Heating harnesses its south exposure for warmth from the sun, delicately balancing solar radiation, making any technology for heating unnecessary.

Nonetheless, technology is celebrated and applied where necessary. Therefore, in //009 Tinizong all inaccuracies of an old crooked barn are 3D-scanned with LiDAR and drone mapping technology, taking advantage of the engineering marvels of our time where necessary.

Seeking the most simple, spectacular version.

AHADIA is an architectural design studio focused on creating sustainable, harmonious spaces that blend with their natural surroundings. We believe in the power of natural materials, particularly wood, to create structures that are both beautiful and environmentally responsible.

Our approach combines traditional craftsmanship with innovative design thinking, resulting in buildings that respect their context while pushing boundaries. Each project is a unique exploration of what's possible when we work with nature rather than against it.

Founded in 2018, our studio has completed residential and commercial projects across Europe, with a particular focus on Alpine and rural environments. We are committed to creating architecture that will stand the test of time, both physically and aesthetically.
